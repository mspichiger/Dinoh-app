How to Use DINOH

DINOH (Digital Innovation Hub) is your central place at Roche to discover and share self-made apps and tools. Browse community-built applications ranked by cost/benefit, find ready-to-use prompts, and submit your own creations to the Container-as-a-Service hosting platform.

What are DINOH Apps?

Apps on DINOH are self-built tools shared by Roche colleagues, hosted on the Container-as-a-Service (CaaS) platform. Every app is rated by the community on its cost/benefit ratio, so you can quickly find what really helps your workflow.

HOW TO USE AN APP:
  1. Browse the cards or use the search to find an app
  2. Click "View Details" to learn more about what it does
  3. Open the app directly and start using it — no setup required

What is the Top Rated section?

Top Rated highlights the highest scoring apps of the week based on the community's cost/benefit ratings. Apps that reach the top of the leaderboard earn a Golden Tier badge — a quick way to spot the most valued tools in the hub.

HOW TO EXPLORE TOP RATED:
  1. Click "Top Rated" in the sidebar to jump to the section
  2. Check the Golden Tier badge and rank of each app
  3. Open an app and rate it yourself to help others discover it

What is the Prompt Library?

The Prompt Library collects reusable prompts shared by Roche colleagues for Gemini, Google Docs, Sheets, Slides and other AI tools. They help you get consistent, high-quality outputs for common tasks without writing prompts from scratch.

HOW TO USE A PROMPT:
  1. Open the Prompt Library from the sidebar
  2. Pick a prompt and copy the text
  3. Paste it into your AI tool and adjust the placeholders
  4. Run the prompt and refine the result if needed
